About — Studio · Est. 2024

Two people. One app a year. That's it.

Nordic Theory Labs is a Stockholm software studio. We make a small number of products with unreasonable care, write what we learn in between, and try not to ship things we wouldn't use ourselves.

Why we made a studio.

We met at a company that shipped fast and apologised slowly. We left because we wanted to spend more time on fewer things — to take a problem we cared about, study it for as long as it needed, and then put one good thing into the world. Not three okay things. Not a platform. One.

The first thing we picked was the Swedish driving theory test. It is a small, well-defined problem with a lot of bad existing software around it, and it personally annoyed both of us. We spent fourteen months on it. 48,000 students have now passed using it.

That is the only thing we have shipped. We are okay with that. The studio is built so we never have to ship anything else until it is also worth shipping.

04 — How we work

Four principles we keep coming back to.

01

Ship less.

One app a year. No platform, no expansion roadmap, no second product until the first is finished. Restraint is a feature.

02

No dark patterns.

No streaks designed to manipulate. No notifications begging for re-engagement. No paywalls behind common sense. You can leave at any time.

03

Write what we learn.

If a decision was hard, the post-mortem is published. The blog is not marketing; it is an obligation of the studio.

04

No outside capital.

Funded by Körkort Hero, by us, and by a small grant from Vinnova in 2024. We answer to the people who use the app, not to anyone else.
