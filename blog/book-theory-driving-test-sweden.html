Booking theory and driving tests in Sweden: how to plan Trafikverket dates

Booking the Swedish category B tests is not only about finding the first available date. Your theory test, driving test, risk training, photo and ID all need to fit together.

The theory test result is valid for 4 months after a pass. During that period, you also need to pass the driving test. If you do not, you must take the theory test again.

Before booking

Make sure your learner's permit is valid and that risk training is completed and registered. If anything is missing, your plan can fail even if you feel ready.

Check the current Trafikverket booking rules before you choose dates. Fees, payment routines and available locations can change.

Theory test first

For category B, the theory test is usually taken before the driving test. The result comes directly after the test. If you pass, you can focus fully on the driving test inside the validity period.

Do not book the theory test too early if your driving is far behind. A passed theory test is useful only if you can realistically pass the driving test within 4 months.

Driving test timing

Book the driving test when your driving is stable in varied traffic, not only on familiar roads. You should be comfortable with roundabouts, lane changes, speed adaptation, parking, vulnerable road users and independent decisions.

If your driving school helps with booking, ask them to explain the plan clearly. If you book yourself, leave enough margin for preparation and travel.

Photo and ID

Trafikverket normally takes your photo before the first knowledge test. You also need valid identification on test day. Arrive early so photo, check-in and nerves do not collide.

Common mistakes

The biggest mistake is booking because a date is available, not because you are ready. Another mistake is passing theory and then waiting too long to book driving.

A good plan connects training level, test availability and the 4-month validity window. The goal is not only to book. The goal is to book at the right moment.

Practical quality checklist

This page is most useful when you connect it to real driving practice. For this topic, keep the main focus clear: A practical planning guide for booking the Swedish category B theory test and driving test with Trafikverket.

  • Before you start, name the exact risk you are training today and where it usually appears in Swedish traffic.
  • During practice, connect the theory rule to observation, speed, road position and communication with other road users.
  • After practice, write down one situation that felt late or uncertain and decide what earlier clue you should notice next time.
  • When reviewing practice questions, explain why the safe answer is safe instead of only remembering the correct option.

Official check
