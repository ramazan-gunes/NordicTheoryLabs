Qiimaha Shatiga Wadista Sweden: Lacagtu Xaggee Ku Baxdaa?

Sharaxaad ku saabsan kharashyada shatiga category B ee Sweden: official fees, eye test, risk courses, driving lessons, retakes iyo qorsheyn.

21-May-2026 8 min Nordic Theory Labs

Qiimaha shatiga wadista Sweden: lacagtu xaggee ku baxdaa?

Qiimaha shatiga category B ee Sweden aad ayuu u kala duwanaan karaa. Labo qof ayaa isla imtixaannada geli kara laakiin lacag kala duwan bixin kara. Qaar ka mid ah kharashyada waa official fees, laakiin qaybta ugu weyn badanaa waxay ku xiran tahay inta training aad u baahan tahay.

Qoraalkani wuxuu sharxayaa cost items-ka ugu waaweyn. Fees iyo prices way is beddeli karaan, sidaas darteed mar walba ka hubi Trafikverket, Transportstyrelsen, optician iyo trafikskola.

Maxay muhiim u tahay?

Dad badan waxay rabaan hal total price. Taasi marar badan ma caawiso. Waxaa fiican inaad kala saarto: official tests, licence production, eye test, risk training, theory material, driving lessons iyo retakes.

Markaad qaybaha aragto, waxaad fahmaysaa waxa la xakameyn karo. Trafikverket theory fee ma beddeli kartid, laakiin lessons aan loo baahnayn waad yareyn kartaa haddii aad si joogto ah u practice gareyso.

Kharashku sidee ayuu u samaysmaa?

Trafikverket wuxuu qaadaa lacag theory test, driving test, photo iyo car hire haddii loo baahdo. Transportstyrelsen wuxuu qaadaa licence production fee. Qaybaha kale waa market prices: optician, driving schools, risk course providers iyo study tools.

Learners badan official fees-ku waa qayb yar. Lessons iyo retakes ayaa noqon kara qaybta qaaliga ah.

Qaybaha kharashka

Learner's permit iyo eye test

Learner's permit badanaa ma aha kharashka weyn, laakiin eye test waxaa la siiyaa provider-ka sameeya. Qiimaha wuu ku kala duwan yahay meelaha.

Haddii medical certificate loo baahdo, kharashku wuu kordhi karaa. Badanaa category B learners waxay u baahan yihiin eye test caadi ah.

Introductory course

Haddii aad rabto private practice, adiga iyo supervisor-ka waa inaad dhamaysaan introductory course. Private practice waxay badbaadin kartaa lacag mustaqbalka, laakiin setup-ka wuu leeyahay kharash.

Theory study

Theory wuxuu noqon karaa book, app, online questions ama course. Kharashkani badanaa wuu ka yar yahay lessons, laakiin theory daciif ah wuxuu keeni karaa retakes qaali ah.

Riskettan iyo risktvåan

Risk education waa qasab category B. Riskettan iyo risktvåan waxaa la siiyaa approved providers, prices-kuna way kala duwan yihiin. Risktvåan badanaa way qaali badan tahay sababtoo ah facility iyo practical exercises ayey u baahan tahay.

Ha dhigin courses-kan waqtiga ugu dambeeya.

Driving lessons

Driving lessons badanaa waa kharashka ugu weyn ee is beddela. Number-ka lessons wuxuu ku xiran yahay experience hore, private practice, traffic judgement iyo sida aad u fahanto Swedish test expectations.

Driver khibrad leh wuxuu u baahan karaa lessons yar, laakiin habits Swedish ah ayuu u baahan karaa. Beginner wuxuu u baahan karaa saacado badan.

Theory test, photo iyo driving test

Trafikverket price list 2026 wuxuu dhigaa category B theory test 420 SEK daytime iyo 520 SEK after 6 pm ama weekends. Category B driving test waa 1000 SEK daytime iyo 1400 SEK after 6 pm ama weekends. Photo when taking a test waa 120 SEK. Car hire category B waa 800 SEK.

Qiimahan waxaa la hubiyay 21 May 2026, laakiin booking ka hor mar kale hubi.

Licence production

Transportstyrelsen wuxuu qaadaa production fee. Boggiisa English wuxuu sheegaa 375 SEK for producing a new licence. Hubi lacagta hadda jirta ka hor payment.

Kharashyada qarsoon

Retakes waa kharash qarsoon oo weyn. Haddii theory ama driving test lagu dhaco, ma aha fee keliya; waxaa iman kara lessons dheeraad ah, travel iyo waiting.

Travel sidoo kale waa kharash. Haddii risk course ama test centre fog yahay, transport iyo waqtigu way kici karaan.

Plan beddeliddu sidoo kale way qaali noqon kartaa. Private practice aan nidaamsanayn waxay keeni kartaa paid corrections dambe.

Dadka cusub iyo migrants

Haddii aad hore u waday, budget-ka u arag adaptation, ma aha basic driving oo keliya. Sweden waxay xoogga saartaa observation, speed discipline iyo cyclists.

Haddii Swedish-ku kuuusan xoog badnayn, waqti iyo budget yar u dhig vocabulary iyo multilingual study support.

Qaladaadka ugu badan

Qalad weyn waa in official fees oo keliya la xisaabiyo. Dadka badankood training ayaa ah kharashka weyn.

Qalad kale waa tools badan oo la iibsado laakiin aan la isticmaalin. Hal system oo maalin kasta la isticmaalo ayaa ka fiican subscriptions badan.

Qalad saddexaad waa test la book gareeyo adigoon diyaar ahayn. Retake wuxuu kordhiyaa lacag iyo stress.

Sidee Körkort Hero u caawin karaa?

Körkort Hero wuxuu kaa caawin karaa theory joogto ah iyo weak areas in la arko ka hor inta aysan qaali noqon. Kuma xirna Trafikverket ama Transportstyrelsen, mana beddelo lessons ama official info.

Soo koobid

Total cost badanaa wuxuu ku xiran yahay training need. Qorshee eye test, private practice setup, theory tools, risk courses, lessons, Trafikverket tests, photo, car hire, licence production iyo retake buffer.

Su'aalo

Maxaa ugu qaalisan?

Badanaa driving lessons, sababtoo ah number-ku aad ayuu u kala duwan yahay.

Official fees ma yihiin kharashka oo dhan?

Maya, waa qayb keliya.

Private practice lacag ma badbaadisaa?

Haa, haddii supervisor approved yahay practice-kuna nidaamsan yahay.

Qiimaha xagee ka hubiyaa?

Trafikverket tests iyo photo, Transportstyrelsen licence production, providers local ah courses.

Liiska hubinta tayada tababarka

Maqaalkani wuxuu waxtar badan leeyahay marka lagu xiro tababar wadis dhab ah. Diiradda ugu weyn ha ahaato: Sharaxaad ku saabsan kharashyada shatiga category B ee Sweden: official fees, eye test, risk courses, driving lessons, retakes iyo qorsheyn.

  • Kahor intaadan bilaabin, sheeg khatarta aad maanta tababaranayso iyo meesha ay badanaa ka muuqato taraafikada Sweden.
  • Inta lagu jiro tababarka, xeerka ku xiriiri fiirinta, xawaaraha, booska waddada iyo xiriirka dadka kale ee waddada isticmaala.
  • Tababarka ka dib, qor xaalad kaa daahday ama aan kuu caddayn, kadib dooro calaamadda aad marka xigta hore u arki doonto.
  • Markaad eegayso su’aalaha tijaabada, sharax sababta jawaabta ammaan ahi u tahay ammaan halkii aad kaliya xafidi lahayd doorashada saxda ah.

Tusaale tababar gaaban

Kadib isla waddada ku celi oo eeg in go’aamadaadu noqdeen kuwo hore, deggan oo si fudud loo sharxi karo.
