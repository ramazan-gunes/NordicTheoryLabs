Subscriptions, payments, refunds.

In short

Apple sells our subscriptions. Apple handles refunds. We don't process your card or see what you spent.

What the subscription unlocks

A Körkort Hero subscription unlocks the full question bank, all 6 study modes, all 12 interface languages, and unlimited mock-exam attempts. A free tier covers a sample of the content so you can decide before subscribing.

Current pricing

Prices are displayed in your local App Store currency and may change. The most up-to-date prices always live on the App Store listing. The app currently offers a recurring subscription with a free trial; lifetime access may be available depending on region.

Auto-renewal

Recurring subscriptions auto-renew through Apple at the end of each period unless cancelled at least 24 hours before renewal. You can cancel at any time in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Refunds

All payments are processed by Apple. To request a refund, follow Apple's process at reportaproblem.apple.com. We do not have authority to issue refunds for App Store transactions, but we will support reasonable requests when contacted by Apple.

Lifetime — what it means

"Lifetime" refers to the lifetime of the Körkort Hero product, not the user's natural lifetime. If we ever discontinue the product, we will give existing lifetime subscribers at least 12 months' notice.
