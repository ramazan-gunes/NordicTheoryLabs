How we build

Four principles, no exceptions.

These are the rules we apply to every product, before we write a single line of code.

Privacy by design

No accounts, no ads, no tracking pixels, no profile of you. We collect the minimum needed to deliver the service — and we tell you exactly what that is.

Offline first

Our apps run on your device, with your data on your device. The internet is a nice-to-have, not a requirement. Your study session shouldn’t depend on a signal bar.

Multilingual from day one

Software made for the Nordics has to meet people in the language they think in. Every app we build ships in a wide set of languages, treated as first-class — not afterthoughts.

Honest by default

Clear pricing, no dark patterns, no fake urgency. If a feature isn’t ready, we say so. If you want to cancel, the path is one tap, not three screens.
