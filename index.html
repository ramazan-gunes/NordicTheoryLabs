03 — Approach

Four rules we keep coming back to.

01

Restraint over noise.

Most apps shout. Ours speak once, clearly, then get out of the way. We obsess over what to leave out.

Editorial · UX
02

Real users, not vanity metrics.

We don't measure success in DAUs. We measure it in driver's licenses earned, books finished, kilometers run.

Measurement
03

Quietly engineered.

Native where it matters. Local-first. Offline by default. Sync is a feature, never a requirement.

Engineering
04

Ship slowly. Refine forever.

Two products a year, not twenty. We'd rather make something we'll be proud of in a decade.

Cadence
