11 · FAQ

Honest answers.

A few questions we hear most often. If yours isn't here, write to us.

Is Körkort Hero affiliated with Trafikverket?

No. We are an independent study app. Trafikverket is the Swedish Transport Administration; they run the official exam. We model our questions on their published format.

Do questions match the real exam?

They are modelled on the official format — same length, same difficulty curve, same wording style. We refresh the pool every term as the curriculum updates.

How much does it cost?

A monthly membership is the equivalent of one driving lesson. No ads, no upsells, no tracking. Cancel anytime in one tap.

Which licence categories are covered?

Car (B) is fully covered. BE, A, A1, AM and Truck B are in the app with progressively fewer questions — we keep expanding.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Download once, study on the train, sync when you're home. Local-first by default.

What if I fail my real exam?

Your subscription stays active. Use the wrong-answer pool to focus, take another mock, retake the exam. We don't disappear after exam day.
