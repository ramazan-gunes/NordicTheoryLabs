A Nordic Theory Labs product

Pass the Swedish driving theory exam — in your language.

Körkort Hero is a complete preparation app for Sweden’s B-class driving theory test. Over 1,000 practice questions, the full theory book, an exam simulator, and a smart wrong-answer trainer — available offline, ad-free, and in 12 languages.

What’s inside

Everything you need to study, in one focused app.

No bloat, no upsells in the middle of a quiz, no distractions. Just the tools that move the needle on test day.

1,000+ practice questions

Authentic-style questions covering the full Swedish theory syllabus, organized by topic so you always know where you stand.

The full theory book

Every chapter of the official theory you need to learn, written for clarity and translated into 12 languages.

Exam simulator

A timed mock exam that mirrors the real Trafikverket test format and pacing, so you walk in knowing what to expect.

Smart wrong-answer trainer

The questions you missed automatically come back, scheduled to surface again at the right time — until you’ve got them.

Flashcards for road signs

Master every Swedish road sign with flip-card practice and spaced repetition. Sign recognition that sticks.

Progress and stats

See accuracy by topic, study streaks, and how close you are to test-ready. The numbers stay on your device.

Built multilingual

Twelve languages, all first-class.

Every question, every sign, every chapter — translated for daily study, not auto-generated for marketing.

Pricing

Start free. Upgrade when you’re serious about the test.

The basics are free, with no ads. Premium unlocks the full question bank, full exam simulator, and smart trainer — managed via Apple, Google, or Microsoft, cancellable any time.

Legal

What we do (and don’t) collect.

Körkort Hero requires no account. Your study data stays on your device. The only data leaving the app is purchase information (handled by RevenueCat) if you subscribe, and anonymized crash reports (Sentry) to help us fix bugs. We don’t sell your data. We don’t show ads. We don’t profile you.
