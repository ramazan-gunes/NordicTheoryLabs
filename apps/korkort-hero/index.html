They are modelled on the official format — same length, same difficulty curve, same wording style. We refresh the pool every term as the curriculum updates.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Körkort Hero?

Körkort Hero is a Swedish driving theory app designed to help you pass the Trafikverket theory test. It includes 1,287 official-format questions across all 10 categories, 154 theory chapters, and supports 12 languages. Built by Nordic Theory Labs in Stockholm.

How many questions does the app have?

Körkort Hero contains 1,287 practice questions modelled on the real Swedish driving theory test, covering all 10 categories including traffic rules, road signs, Riskettan scenarios, vehicle knowledge, and environmental driving.

What languages are supported?

The app supports 12 languages: Swedish, English, Arabic, Farsi, Turkish, Polish, Somali, Russian, Bosnian, Kurdish, Finnish, and Spanish. You can study theory in your own language and then take the exam in Swedish.

Is Körkort Hero free?

Körkort Hero is free to download with access to core features. A premium subscription unlocks the full question bank, adaptive review algorithm, and all 154 theory chapters in your language.

Does the app cover Riskettan (risk awareness)?

Yes. Körkort Hero includes comprehensive Riskettan practice covering risk awareness scenarios, hazard perception, and the specific question formats used in the risk education portion of the Swedish driving license process.

Can I study offline?

Yes, Körkort Hero works fully offline. All questions, theory chapters, and your progress are stored locally on your device. Internet sync is optional and never required.

How is this different from other körkort apps?

Körkort Hero is designed with Nordic restraint — no gamification, no streaks, no nagging notifications. Our adaptive algorithm identifies your weakest areas and serves you only the questions you need, so you spend less time studying and more time feeling confident.

Pass the Swedish Driving Theory Test with Confidence

The Swedish driving theory test (kunskapsprov) administered by Trafikverket requires a deep understanding of traffic rules, road signs, risk awareness, and vehicle operation. Körkort Hero is built specifically for this exam, with questions that mirror the official format and difficulty level.

Whether you're a new resident learning Swedish road signs for the first time, or a young driver preparing for your B-körkort, our app adapts to your knowledge level. The built-in spaced repetition system ensures you review difficult questions at optimal intervals — helping you retain information longer with less effort.

Complete Coverage of All Test Categories

Körkort Hero covers every category of the Swedish theory test: traffic rules and regulations, road signs and markings, the vehicle and its properties, the human factor, environment and energy, Riskettan (risk awareness training), driving in different conditions, first aid, and special driving situations.

Each of our 154 theory chapters is written in clear, accessible language and available in all 12 supported languages. Practice questions are refreshed each term to stay current with any changes to the official exam content.
