# Pedestrians and cyclists in Sweden

Pedestrians and cyclists are vulnerable road users.

A safe driver gives them extra attention because a small car mistake can have serious consequences.

In Swedish towns, look carefully before turning, opening gaps, passing parked cars and approaching crossings.

Cyclists can be fast and may be hidden by buses, parked cars or darkness.

Pedestrians may step out late, especially near schools, bus stops and shops.

On the driving test, the examiner wants to see early observation, lower speed and clear margins around people.
