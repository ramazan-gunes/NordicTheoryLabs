# Priority rules in Sweden

Priority rules are a major part of Swedish driving.

You need to understand signs, road markings and general rules.

Give way means you must slow down or stop if needed and let traffic on the priority road pass.

Stop means a full stop, even if the road looks empty.

The right-hand rule can apply at unsigned intersections, but signs and signals override it.

Many learners fail because they hesitate too long or enter too early.

Train by naming the rule before each junction: sign, signal, road marking or right-hand rule.
