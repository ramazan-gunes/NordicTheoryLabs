# Driving school or private practice in Sweden

Many learners in Sweden use both driving school and private practice.

The best result usually comes from using them for different purposes.

Driving school is strong for structure, feedback, safety-critical skills and test readiness.

Private practice is strong for repetition, confidence and time behind the wheel.

Do not let private practice become random driving.

Ask the instructor what to practise at home, then repeat the same skill calmly with your handledare.

If the learner receives conflicting advice, use the driving instructor as the main reference.

A good balance saves money without weakening safety.
