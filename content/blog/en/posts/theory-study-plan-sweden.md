How to build a realistic weekly plan for rules, risk, signs and mixed practice. Use it to connect theory, practice and safe decisions before the Swedish driving test.

## How to practise

Practise it in short sessions, then repeat it in mixed traffic until the decision feels calm.

Use real examples from lessons, private practice and practice tests. Ask what you saw first, what you predicted and what you changed before the risk became close.

## Common mistakes

Most learner mistakes come from late observation, rushing and treating the rule as isolated theory.

A better routine is to slow down the situation mentally: look early, choose position, adapt speed and communicate clearly with signals and placement.

## Before the test

On test day, the examiner wants safe judgement, clear planning and steady control.

Official details can change, so always check Trafikverket and Transportstyrelsen before booking or making legal decisions.
