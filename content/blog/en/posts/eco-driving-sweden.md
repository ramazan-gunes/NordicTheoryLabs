# Eco-driving in Sweden

Eco-driving is part of modern Swedish driving education.

It means planning ahead, avoiding unnecessary acceleration, keeping a steady speed and using the car efficiently.

Good eco-driving is also safer because it reduces sudden braking and stressful decisions.

Look far ahead, release the accelerator early, keep distance and avoid racing between red lights.

In the theory test, eco-driving may appear as questions about environment, fuel, tyres, speed and planning.

In the driving test, it shows through calm and predictable driving.
