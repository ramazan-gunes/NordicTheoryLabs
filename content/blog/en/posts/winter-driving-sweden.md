# Winter driving in Sweden: what learners should practise

Swedish winter driving changes everything.

Snow, ice, darkness and wet roads make normal mistakes more serious.

Learners should practise smooth steering, longer safety margins, gentle braking and early planning.

Winter tyres are required in winter road conditions during the official winter period, and current tyre rules should be checked with Transportstyrelsen.

Do not treat winter driving as a separate trick.

It is ordinary safe driving with more patience, more distance and fewer sudden movements.

Practise starts, stops, turns, hill starts and parking in safe places before entering busy traffic.

On test day and in daily driving, the examiner expects speed adaptation and risk awareness, not bravery.
