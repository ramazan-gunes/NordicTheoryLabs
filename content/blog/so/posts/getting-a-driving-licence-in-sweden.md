# Sida loo qaato shatiga wadista Sweden: hagaha category B

Habka shatiga wadista ee Sweden mararka qaar wuu adkaan karaa, gaar ahaan haddii aad dalka ku cusub tahay ama Swedish-ku uusan ahayn luqaddaada xooggan. Laakiin marka tallaabo tallaabo loo kala qaado, wuu fududaadaa.

Qoraalkani wuxuu ka hadlayaa shatiga category B, oo ah shatiga baabuurka caadiga ah. Sharciyada, khidmadaha iyo waqtiyada ballanta way is beddeli karaan, sidaas darteed mar walba ka hubi Transportstyrelsen iyo Trafikverket.

## Habku sidee ayuu u shaqeeyaa?

Transportstyrelsen waxay qaabilsan tahay oggolaanshaha barashada, shuruudaha caafimaadka, qofka kula baraya gaarka ah iyo bixinta shatiga. Trafikverket waxay qaabilsan tahay imtixaanka theory-ga iyo imtixaanka wadista.

Badanaa waxaad bilaabaysaa körkortstillstånd, ka dib baaritaanka indhaha, theory iyo driving practice, riskettan, risktvåan, theory test, kadib driving test.

## 1. Codso körkortstillstånd

körkortstillstånd waa oggolaanshaha aad u baahan tahay ka hor inta aadan bilaabin barashada wadista. Codsiga waxaa lagu sameeyaa Transportstyrelsen, waxaana ku jira health declaration. Baaritaanka indhaha sidoo kale waa loo baahan yahay.

Haddii aad si gaar ah ula baranayso qof qoys ama saaxiib ah, qofkaasi waa inuu noqdaa supervisor la ansixiyay. Adiga iyo supervisor-ka waa inaad dhamaysaan introductory course ka hor practice-ga gaarka ah.

## 2. Baaritaanka indhaha

Synundersökning waa baaritaan lagu xaqiijiyo in araggaagu ku filan yahay wadista. Badanaa waxaa lagu sameeyaa optician ama meel la oggol yahay. Samee waqti hore si codsigaagu uusan u istaagin.

## 3. Theory-ga iyo wadista isla socodsii

Sweden, theory iyo practical driving way isku xiran yihiin. Theory test-ku wuxuu cabbiraa traffic rules, signs, vehicle knowledge, environment, safety iyo driver behaviour. Driving test-ku wuxuu eegaa inaad aqoontaas ku dabaqi karto waddada.

Qalad badan waa in la xifdiyo su'aalo kaliya. Su'aalaha practice-ka ma aha kuwa imtixaanka rasmiga ah. Faham sababta sharcigu u jiro, sida speed, weather, tiredness iyo cyclists ay risk u kordhiyaan.

## 4. Riskettan

Riskettan waa qaybta koowaad ee risk education-ka qasabka ah. Waxay ka hadashaa alcohol, drugs, tiredness iyo risky behaviour. Ma aha kaliya course aad saxdo; waa aasaas ku saabsan responsible driving.

## 5. Risktvåan

Risktvåan waxay ku saabsan tahay speed, safety, road conditions iyo xaalado adag. Dad badan waxay u yaqaanaan skid training, laakiin ujeeddadu waa inaad fahanto sida control-ku u yaraado marka speed iyo waddo simbiriirixan isku yimaadaan.

Labada qaybood waa inay ahaadaan completed oo valid ah markaad galayso imtixaannada. Risk course-ku badanaa wuxuu shaqeeyaa shan sano, laakiin xaaladdaada ka hubi Transportstyrelsen.

## 6. Theory test

Theory test-ka category B waxaa lagu qaadaa Trafikverket. Waxaad u baahan tahay ID sax ah, waxaana lagaa qaadayaa sawir ka hor imtixaanka theory-ga koowaad.

Imtixaanku wuxuu leeyahay 70 multiple-choice questions. Shan su'aalood lama xisaabiyo, markaa score-ka ugu badan waa 65. Waqtigu waa 50 daqiiqo, waxaadna u baahan tahay 52 sax ah. Haddii aad gudubto, natiijadu waxay shaqaysaa afar bilood.

Category B theory test waxaa lagu book-gareyn karaa luqado kale. Haddii aad leedahay reading difficulties ama baahi kale, waxaa laga yaabaa inaad codsan karto extra time, oral test ama interpreter.

## 7. Driving test

Driving test-ku wuxuu tusayaa inaad si ammaan ah, madax-bannaan iyo judgement fiican u wadi karto. Waxaa ku jiri kara safety check, manoeuvres iyo traffic driving.

Driving test lama book-gareeyo luqad kale. Examiner-ku Swedish ayuu ku hadlaa, sidaas darteed baro erayada muhiimka ah: höger, vänster, parkera, vänd, motorväg, landsväg.

Haddii aad ku gasho automatic car, licence-kaaga wuxuu heli karaa condition 78, taas oo ka dhigan automatic oo keliya. Manual waxaad u baahan doontaa driving test cusub.

## Trafikskola mise private practice?

Trafikskola waxay bixisaa structure iyo feedback professional ah. Private practice waxay kordhin kartaa saacadaha wadista waxayna yareyn kartaa kharashka, laakiin waxay u baahan tahay supervisor deggan oo sharciyada fahmaya.

Dad badan waxay ku fiican yihiin isku-darka: school si loo helo qaladaadka, private practice si loo celceliyo.

## Waqti iyo kharash

Waqtiga iyo kharashku way kala duwan yihiin. Experience hore, luqad, inta jeer ee aad practice samayso, availability-ga imtixaanka iyo lessons-ka ayaa saameeya. Fees iyo prices way is beddeli karaan.

Ka fikir budget-ka: eye test, permit, introductory course, lessons, riskettan, risktvåan, theory material, theory test, driving test iyo car rental haddii loo baahdo.

## Talooyin haddii Swedish-ku kugu adag yahay

Ku bilow luqadda aad si fiican u fahanto, laakiin baro erayada Swedish-ka ee traffic-ga. Signs, road markings, place names iyo instructions waxay ku imaan karaan Swedish.

## Qaladaadka ugu badan

In imtixaan la book-gareeyo xilli hore, su'aalo la xifdiyo oo keliya, mirrors iyo blind spots la iloobo, cyclists si fiican loo eegin, iyo in theory-ga loo arko memory game waa qaladaad caadi ah.

## Sidee Körkort Hero u caawin karaa?

Körkort Hero wuxuu kaa caawin karaa inaad theory-ga u barato si nidaamsan, luqado badan ku barato, weak areas aragto oo progress-kaaga la socoto. Kuma xirna Trafikverket ama Transportstyrelsen, mana beddelo official information ama driving practice.

## Soo koobid

Qaado learner's permit, samee eye test, theory iyo driving isku wad, dhammee riskettan iyo risktvåan, gudub theory test, kadib driving test. Fahamku wuu ka fiican yahay xifdin keliya.

## Su'aalo badan la is weydiiyo

### Körkortstillstånd ma qasab baa?

Haa, waa qasab ka hor driving practice.

### Risk courses ma qasab baa?

Haa, category B wuxuu u baahan yahay riskettan iyo risktvåan.

### Theory test luqad kale ma lagu qaadan karaa?

Haa, category B waxaa jira luqado kale, laakiin options-ka hadda jira ka hubi Trafikverket.

### Driving test English ama Somali ma noqon karaa?

Driving test lama book-gareeyo luqad kale; examiner-ku Swedish ayuu ku hadlaa.
