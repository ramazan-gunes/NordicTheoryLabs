Changelog — 14 releases since v1.0

What's changed, version by version.

Honest release notes. We write what we shipped, why, and what we cut — not marketing copy disguised as bullet points. Subscribe via RSS for one entry per release, on average twice a month.

New Improved Fixed Removed Security
v2.2.0
14 May 2026
Körkort Hero
iOS 17+Android 12+Web

Weekly review mode & faster offline sync.

A long-requested mode that surfaces the questions you've been wrong about most recently, plus the offline sync rewrite that was overdue. No new chapters in this release — the question pool itself is unchanged.

New Weekly review — a new home-screen tile that surfaces your eight most-recent wrong answers from the week. Tap to spaced-rep them. Opt-in, off by default.
New Quiet morning mode — a 15-minute calm session for the day of the test. No streaks, no scoreboards, only review.
Improved Offline sync rewritten from the ground up — first sync after install is now ~3× faster on slow trains north of Sundsvall (which is where we noticed).
Improved Arabic typography rebuilt — proper kashida justification and ligature handling. Thanks to R. Hammad for the patch.
Fixed Question 318 (motorway entry / yield) had the wrong correct answer flagged in Tigrinya. Corrected and re-scored for affected users.
Fixed Dark mode on iOS no longer flashes white during the initial splash on cold launch.
Removed Daily streak counter removed entirely (was opt-in since v2.0). Internal A/B showed it pushed people to over-study. Post-mortem on the blog ↗
1,420 users in beta 14 days of TestFlight 3 known issues open

v2.1.0
02 Apr 2026
Körkort Hero
iOSAndroidWeb

Two new languages: Tigrinya & Dari.

In partnership with Sveriges Stadsmissioner and three local refugee-aid groups. Both languages required a full re-edit of the question pool rather than machine translation — translation took 11 weeks.

New Tigrinya question pool, 823 items, voice-over included.
New Dari question pool, 823 items, voice-over included.
Improved Language picker now sorts by script, then by name. Makes Pashto / Dari / Persian easier to tell apart at a glance.
Fixed Right-to-left bug in the "wrong answers" card where the explanation overlapped the question header.
12 languages total 11 weeks of translation 3 NGOs reviewed
v2.0.0
11 Mar 2026
Körkort Hero
iOSAndroidWeb

The big quiet release.

One year on from v1.0. The schedule, the question explanations, and the way we measure progress are all new. No new features by count — but most of what's on screen is reworked.

New Adaptive scheduling rewritten on the simplified curve described in our spaced-repetition essay ↗.
New Every wrong answer now opens with a 2–3 sentence explanation written by an examiner, not generated.
New iCloud / Google Drive sync, opt-in. End-to-end encrypted with your device passcode.
Improved Progress view rewritten — shows readiness instead of percent completed. We think this is a better signal.
Removed Public leaderboards, deleted entirely. They didn't help anyone pass.
